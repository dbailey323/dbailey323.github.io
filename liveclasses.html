Dance&Tone Express


Mondays @ 6.30pm


Mountsorrel Memorial Centre


DANCE&TONE is a 30 minute dance class set to current and chart music with a variety of dance styles designed to help you lose weight, tone up and increase your fitness levels.

The fun atmosphere means you won’t even realise you are exercising!


Strong&Stretched


Mondays @ 7.00pm


Mountsorrel Memorial Centre


Are you ready to create a shaped, sculpted and toned body? All whilst lengthening muscles and releasing the stress on everyday? Helping to prevent injuries and helping you to feel powerful and connected to yourself? Low impact, high burn?

Then STRONG&STRETCHED is here! 30 minutes to blast through some stretch and conditioning exercises where we speed up and hold movements to make your muscles shake and burn followed by a full body stretch to release the tension and hopefully help those next day DOMS! So don’t think it’s going to be easy.. but it will be worth it!

Fight Fierce


Thursdays @ 7.00pm


Methodist Church Centre


FIGHT FIERCE is a martial arts inspired workout set to fresh tunes to let out your inner fierceness. Punch, kick and strike your way to fitness whilst letting out any aggression or stress. Warrior mode is on!!

No martial arts experience necessary and non contact.
